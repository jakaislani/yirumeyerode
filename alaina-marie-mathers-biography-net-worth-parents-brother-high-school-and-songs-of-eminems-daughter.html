Alaina Marie Mathers Biography; Net Worth, Parents, Brother, High School And Songs Of Eminems Dau

Dawn became a drug addict and despite Eminem’s effort to get her out of addiction, she couldn’t get out of addiction hence, he adopted Alaina to provide her with a better upbringing.
