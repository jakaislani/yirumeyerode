Will Levis' girlfriend Gia Duddy hit with 2023 NFL Draft questions

Could former Kentucky quarterback Will Levis be the first-overall pick in this year’s NFL draft?

That’s what some fans are asking Levis’ girlfriend, Gia Duddy, on Instagram — among other draft-centric questions — as Thursday’s highly anticipated first round nears.

“Will Levis going 1?” one Instagram user commented Tuesday on Duddy’s latest post.

Another responded, “We will be watching on Thursday night.”

Levis, who is widely expected to be selected in the opening round, was recently linked to a Reddit rumor that alleged he had been telling loved ones the Panthers will pick him at No. 1.

The 23-year-old addressed the allegations Wednesday in Kansas City, where this year’s draft is taking place, stating in part to The Athletic, “Don’t believe everything you read on the Internet.”

As for Duddy, who has been dating Levis since 2021, her Instagram followers are eager to see her draft-night look.

“What color outfit for Thursday night?!” one fan exclaimed.

A separate follower expressed, “yellow on youuuuu!!” after the Penn State student modeled a hat in the bright hue.

A social media sensation in her own right, Duddy boasts nearly 60,000 followers on Instagram, where she regularly posts photos of her travels, game-day styles, and cozy snaps with Levis.

Recently, Duddy gave fans a peek at her pre-draft activities on TikTok — where she also boasts an impressive following — as she waited to board her flight to Kansas City.

“KC here i come #nfldraft,” Duddy captioned the video.

Prior to this week’s draft festivities, Levis visited the Patriots.

The 6-foot-4, 229-pound product had previously met with the Raiders, Texans, and Panthers, who are projected to pick former Alabama quarterback Bryce Young at No. 1 overall.
