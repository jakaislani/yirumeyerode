Archana Puran Singh needs no detailed introduction as she has been the most famous film and TV actress of the Hindi cinema and entertainment media industry. She has established herself a great, successful, and bold artist who has been working for long and has been constantly winning the hearts of many with her remarkable acting. She has acted in various movies and TV serials also appeared in TV shows for which she got huge acclaim. She was born on 26th September 1962, Dehradun, India, and recognized as the great actress, TV host, judge, comedian, and model as well. Undoubtedly, she is a multi-skilled person and gained much recognition. She has also worked in Tamil and Punjabi film industry and appeared in various movies.

Career

She completed her studies from Delhi University. Later in 1980, she shifted to Mumbai and started working as a model. Since then, she modeled for various notable brands and got immense love from die-hard fans. In 1982, she made her acting debut with a movie named “Nikaah” in which she appeared as a salesgirl. Later, in 1987, she appeared in the movie “Jalwaa” in which Archana Singh worked alongside the veteran actor Naseeruddin Shah. Later, she started appearing in various supporting roles for which she became famous. One of the best-loved roles she played in the movie “Kuch Kuch Hota Hai” starring the big names of the industry including superstar Shahrukh Khan, Rani Mukherjee, Kajol, Farida Jalal, and Anupam Kher played significant roles. In the 1990s, she appeared in some popular movies including “Saudagar” starring Pooja Bhatt, “Agneepath” starring Ajay Devgan and Tabu, “Raja Hindustani” starring Amir Khan and Karishma Kapoor while Archana Singh and Johny Lever played amazing roles in the movie and go much acclaim. While she has acted in a large number of movies and also did some item number, like; mukabaaz and others.

Since the 2000s, Archana Singh has been seen in comedy roles and some of her popular roles in the movies “Bol Bachchan”, “De Dana Dan”, “Dolly Ki Doli”, and other film projects are worth sharing to watch.

Besides, she also marked her identity in the silver screen and also acted in various TV serials and appeared on shows. In 1985, she made her TV debut “Karamchand” and for this, she became famous. While another project includes “Nehle Pe Dehla”. In the show, she worked alongside her real-life husband Parmeet Sethi in this show. Moreover, she took part in a variety of reality shows as contestant including “Nach Baliye” in 2005, and later, she hosted the dance reality show “Jhalak Dikhlaja” in which she appeared alongside husband Gurmeet Singh. She also appeared in the Kapil Sharma Show and Comedy Circus as a Judge and enthralled a number of audiences over there from 2008 to 2014.
