Abby Eden, an accomplished journalist and anchor, began her career with various small jobs before finding her path in the media industry.

She took on roles as a sandwich artist at Blimpse and worked as a server at six different restaurants.

While pursuing her dream of becoming a reporter, she juggled two other jobs: a tanning salon receptionist and a substitute teacher.

After university, Abby started her journey at KOMU-TV, gaining experience in various aspects of media, including photography, editing, production, and anchoring.

Additionally, she interned for a non-profit organization in Buenos Aires.

Her first breakthrough in the field was as an anchor and reporter for KETK-TV and KFX, where she spent three fulfilling years.

Later, she became a multimedia journalist at WDAF-TV in Kansas City, showcasing her versatility in different media formats.

Abby joined FOX 4 in 2011 at 38 in a significant career move. This marked a pivotal moment in her journey as she took on the role of a weekday anchor.

Is Abby Eden Pregnant 2023? Baby Bump Rumours

No, Abby Eden is not pregnant in 2023. She had her last child delivered on October 14, 2021.

Abby Eden, the award-winning host, is happily married to engineer Tyler Curry.

In April 2016, Tyler Curry, Abby’s long-term boyfriend, proposed, and she joyfully accepted, announcing their engagement on Facebook with a picture.

Later that year, on November 26, 2016, they married in an intimate wedding ceremony at Our Lady of Sorrows Catholic Church in Kansas City.

Their marriage has been blessed with three beautiful children. The eldest, Dorothy Ann, was born in May 2018. In 2020, they welcomed their second son, Atticus.

And in October 2021, Abby shared the happy news of expecting another baby boy, Ellis Benedict, who was born on October 14, 2021.

Throughout her pregnancy, Abby kept her fans updated on social media, and they showered her with love and well-wishes.

Fortunately, the pregnancy went smoothly, and both Abby and Tyler were overjoyed to welcome their third child into the world.

When Is Abby Eden Coming Back To Fox 4?

Abby Eden is returning to Fox 4 on Monday, September 25, 2023. The channel is delighted to announce her comeback.

Eden, the Morning Show Anchor to WDAF-TV FOX4, will rejoin the dynamic morning show team, including Pat McGonigle, Karli Ritter, and Nick Vasos.

The morning show is regarded as the most-watched newscast in Kansas City.

Expressing her excitement, Abby Eden shared that she missed being in journalism and that being a journalist has been her lifelong passion since she was a little girl.

She looks forward to continuing the vital work that FOX4 does for their viewers.

FOX4’s Vice President and FM, Tracy Brogden Miller, expressed immense joy about Eden’s return, stating they are thrilled to have her back with the FOX4 family.

Abby’s history of dedicated service to viewers and the community makes her a valuable addition to the special team.

With Abby reunited, the FOX4 morning show lineup now consists of Nick Vasos, Pat McGonigle, Karli Ritter, Kristen Holloway, Kerri Stowell, and Michelle Bogowith.

This formidable team has maintained a dominant position in Kansas City’s ratings from 6 am to 10 am for over a decade.
