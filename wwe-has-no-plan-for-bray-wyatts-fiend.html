WWE Has No Plan For Bray Wyatts Fiend

Bray Wyatt disappeared from WWE television on the road to WrestleMania 39. Now, fans are waiting for his next move in the pro wrestling world. He’s still with WWE, and there is a bit more information as we continue waiting.

Bray Wyatt has been absent from WWE programming due to health-related reasons. However, there has been growing speculation about the potential return of The Eater of Worlds to WWE in recent weeks.

According to recent reports, there is a possibility that Bray Wyatt might make his comeback on the road to SummerSlam 2023. Rumors have also made the rounds that WWE intended to reintroduce Wyatt’s Fiend character.

Now Jason Baker, who is the makeup effects artist and collaborator with WWE that worked on masks for Wyatt, took to Twitter to dispel those rumors. It seems that WWE has no plans to revive The Fiend.

Jason Baker clarified that there are no plans for The Fiend’s return, putting an end to the speculation surrounding Wyatt’s potential resurrection as the menacing character.

“That might be a little difficult considering that all of the mask molds were destroyed last year…”

Bray Wyatt’s WWE status has a lot of attention. There are a lot of eyes on everything he does, but he hasn’t made a return to the ring yet. In fact, they destroyed his mask.

Alexa Bliss will not be factored into anything Bray Wyatt did. Ringside News reported that Bliss’ absence has nothing to do with Bray Wyatt’s disappearance. Now we know that she is pregnant, and that is the reason why she hasn’t been seen.
