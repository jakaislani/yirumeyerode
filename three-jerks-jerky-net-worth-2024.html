Three Jerks Jerky: The Beginning

The founders of Three Jerks Jerky, Jordan Barrocas and Daniel Fogelson, started the company with a simple idea: to create a high-quality, flavorful beef jerky that stands out from the rest. Appearance on Shark Tank

In 2015, Three Jerks Jerky appeared on the popular TV show Shark Tank, where they secured a deal with Daymond John. This exposure significantly boosted the company’s sales and popularity, contributing to its increasing net worth.

Partnership with Rastelli Foods Group
