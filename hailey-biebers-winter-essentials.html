Shop Hailey Bieber's Winter Essentials 2024

1

For a Hydrating Cleanse

CeraVe Moisturizing Non-Foaming Face Wash

While you might assume that most of Mrs. Bieber's beauty tips are out of your budget, we're happy to report that some of her favorite skincare products are surprisingly affordable. During a 2023 interview with The Cut, she revealed she's a big fan of budget products such as CeraVe's Hydrating Facial Cleanser. "It's an O.G., a forever go-to brand for me," she told the publication, and it's easy to see why.

The cleanser helps hydrate skin thanks to the inclusion of hyaluronic acid, while three essential ceramides work their magic to restore the skin's natural barrier.

Winter Skincare Must-Have

SkinMedica HA5 Rejuvenating Hydrator

Hailey Bieber has made a habit of sharing her skincare favorites on social media. For instance, the model praised SkinMedica's HA5 Rejuvenating Hydrator in an Instagram Story (via Glamour), writing, "I just recently started this one. It's helped keep my skin from getting chapped in the cold."

Bieber's winter skincare must-have promotes the reduction of fine lines and wrinkles, with noticeable improvements occurring after eight weeks of use. Meanwhile, hyaluronic acid encourages the overall rejuvenation of healthy skin.

Your Go-To Brightening Serum

beautystat cosmetics Universal C Skin Refiner

Hailey Bieber shared her love of BeautyStat's Universal C Skin Refiner during a video posted to her own YouTube channel, in which she celebrated some of her favorite Black-owned businesses.

Best Skincare Device

For Calming Irritation and Redness

Tower 28 Beauty SOS Daily Rescue Facial Spray

Another product that Hailey Bieber has lauded on social media is Tower 28 Beauty's SOS Daily Rescue Facial Spray, which she shared on her Instagram Stories (via StyleCaster). For Gentle Exfoliation

MARA Chia + Moringa Algae Enzyme Cleansing Oil

When Hailey Bieber shared her beauty secrets with Allure, she advised her fans to always double-cleanse their skin. In the video, she could be seen using MARA's Chia + Moringa® Algae Enzyme Cleansing Oil, which gently exfoliates the skin, removes makeup, and aids hydration.

The cleansing oil features a plethora of fruit enzymes, including pumpkin, pineapple, and papaya, which brighten the skin while exfoliating. The product is also packed with nutrients and omegas thanks to the inclusion of a number of superfoods.

For Enviable Dewy Skin
