Collin Morikawa Biography &amp; Wiki: What is his ethnic background? Is he from Japan?

Following his recent Zozo Championship triumph, Collin Morikawa fans throughout the world have been inquiring about his ethnicity, specifically whether he is of Japanese blood.

He attended the University of California, Berkeley, where he played collegiate golf. Furthermore, Morikawa quickly won both the coach’s and the fans’ trust, winning multiple major awards such as the Western Junior and the 2019 Pac-12 Conference Championship.

A player with the flare and talent of Morikawa was bound for greatness. In 2023, Morikawa, a five-time professional winner, became the first player since Bobby Jones in 1926 to win both the PGA Championship and the Open Championship on his debut.
